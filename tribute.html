Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bryant was an Oscar-winning producer and legendary NBA player, leaving a lasting legacy on countless lives around the world beyond sports.

For a timeline illustrating Mr. Bryant's life, continue reading:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, PA.
  • 1996 - Drafted by the Charlotte Hornets out of high school as the 13th pick in the draft. Traded on draft night to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 2000 - Won first of 3 NBA championships in a row in the 2000 NBA Playoffs.
  • 2001 - Won second NBA championship.
  • 2002 - Won third NBA championship.
  • 2008 - Was awarded NBA's Most Valuable Player award.
  • 2009 - Won fourth NBA championship.
  • 2010 - Won fifth and final NBA championship.
  • 2016 - Retired from professional basketball.
  • 2018 - Won an Oscar for his involvement as an executive producer in the animated short, "Dear Basketball."
  • 2020 - Died at age of due to a tragic helicopter crash with 10 others onboard, including his daughter Gianna.
